Plain of Six Glaciers Hike

The Six Glaciers hike is a very scenic hike in Banff National Park, Alberta. The outstanding view from the top covers world famous Lake Louis, along with three other neighbouring mountains. Its also a challenging hike being around 14.6 kilometers.

  • Roughly takes an average of four hours and thirty-eight minutes to complete.
  • Starts around 1,734 meters above sea level with the final viewpoint being 2,202 meters high.
  • Difficulty Level is moderate.
